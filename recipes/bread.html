Bread

Bread is a common food that everybody eat almos every day and goes well with many different dishes and moments.

Ingredients

  • 500g of white flour
  • 2 table spoon of salt
  • 7g of yeast
  • 3 table spoon of oil
  • 300ml of water

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, the salt and the yeast in a bowl
  2. Add the oil and the water and mix
  3. Tip onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for around 10 mins
  4. Once the dough is satin-smooth, place it in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with cling film
  5. Let it rest until it doubled it size
  6. Heat the oven to 200 degrees
  7. Bake for 25-30 mins
