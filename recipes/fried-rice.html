Egg Fried Rice

This is a perfect recipe if you don't have much time to prepare a meal.

It's a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan mixed with eggs, vegetables and even seafood or meat.

Ingredients

  • 250g of long grain rice
  • 3 Table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 Finely chopped onion
  • 4 Beaten eggs
  • 2 Sliced spring onions

Steps

  1. Cook the rice following the pack instructions and the dry it aside
  2. Heat 2 table spoons of oil in a wok on high heat
  3. Add the onion and cook it for 5 minutes
  4. Add the rice and toast it for 3 minutes and put the mix on the side
  5. Add the remaining oil and cook the eggs
  6. Mix everything
  7. Serve into a bowl
