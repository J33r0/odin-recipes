Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish made with multiple pasta layers. It can be done with many ingredients, such as chicken, meat, or veggies if you want the vegan option.

In this post we will show you how to make the meat lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Meat : sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • 1 or 2 Onions
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Tomato sauce cans
  • 2 Tomato paste cans
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • Spices : parsley, basil, salt, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 Bloc of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles as indicated on the box
  3. Stripe the mozzarella cheese
  4. In an oven container put a layer of meat
  5. Cover with the noodles
  6. Add a layer of mozzarella
  7. Repeat until the container is full

Cooking

Preheat the oven to 190 degrees and cook for 50 minutes
